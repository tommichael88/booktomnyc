Scope of Work

Here are the common services offered by the service provider, grouped by category:

A. Minor Home Repairs

Minor home repairs deal with small-scale repair services that fix or restore various aspects of a home. These services don't need significant reconstruction or renovation but are crucial for maintaining and updating residences.

   SERVICE DESCRIPTION INCLUDED CLIENT
A-1 Door Hardware Installation Installing door handles/locks on doors. Tools, labor, and expertise. Door hardware (knobs, locks, etc.)
A-2 Hollow Door Repair Fixing damage to interior hollow doors. Patching materials, paint, and tools. Access to the damaged door.
A-3 Wall Repair - Patch & Paint Mending minor wall damages. Patching materials, paint, and tools. Access to the damaged wall area.
A-4 Appliance Hourly Service Maintenance, repair, or assessment of household appliances. Diagnostic tools and expertise. Appliance and specific parts.
A-5 Furniture Hourly Service Repairing various furniture types. Repair tools and materials. Furniture piece for repair.

B. Wall Mounting

Wall mounting services focus on securing items to walls, enhancing living or workspace aesthetics.

SERVICE DESCRIPTION INCLUDED CLIENT
B-1 Single Rod Curtain Install Installing curtains or drapes using a single rod. Installation tools and expertise. Curtains, drapes, and rod.
B-2 Item Mounting Hourly Service Mounting frames, shelves, or similar items on walls. Mounting hardware, tools, and expertise. Items to be mounted.
B-3 TV Mounting (up to 60") w/o Cable Management Mounting TVs up to 60" without hiding cables. Basic mounting tools and expertise. TV and mounting bracket.
B-4 TV Mounting (up to 60") + Concealed Cables Mounting TVs up to 60" with hidden cables. Mounting tools, cable concealment, and expertise. TV, bracket, and cables.

Supporting customers with basic plumbing duties under their supervision. Not responsible for project leadership or autonomous decision-making.

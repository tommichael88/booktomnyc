SCOPE OF WORK

Scope of Work

Minor Home Repairs

Door Handles & Locks Installation

Scope of Work: Inspection of the door and hardware provided by the client. Installation of door knob and lock. Testing for proper function and security.

Hollow Door Repair

Scope of Work: Assessment of the interior door damage. Patching of damaged areas. Painting to match the existing door color.

Wall Repair - Patch & Paint

Scope of Work: Identification of damaged areas on the interior wall. Patching and smoothing of affected areas. Painting to seamlessly blend with the existing wall color.

Appliances Hourly Service Rate

Scope of Work: Assessment of appliance condition. Maintenance, repair, or recommendation for specialized service.

Furniture Repair Hourly Service Rate

Scope of Work: See Furniture Fixes.

Wall Mounting

Curtains/Drapes w. Single Rod Installation

Scope of Work: Measurement and marking for rod placement. Installation of curtain or drape rod. Hanging of curtains or drapes and adjustment for proper fall and appearance.

Frame/Shelf Mounting Hourly Service Rate

Scope of Work: Assessment of wall type and condition. Secure mounting of frames or shelves. Ensuring level and stable installation.

TV Mounting (Under 60") without cable management

Scope of Work: Assessment of wall suitability. Secure mounting of TV bracket and TV. Connection of TV to power and input sources.

TV Mounting (Under 60") + Concealed Cables

Scope of Work: Assessment of wall suitability. Secure mounting of TV bracket and TV. Concealment of cables for a clean appearance.

Computer Tech Services

PC Diagnostic Service

Scope of Work: Comprehensive hardware and software diagnostic tests. Identification of issues and recommendation for solutions.

Data Transfer / Data Back-Up

Scope of Work: Backup of existing data to a secure location. Transfer of data to a new device or storage medium.

Part Replacement/Upgrade

Scope of Work: Identification of faulty or outdated hardware parts. Replacement or upgrade with client-provided or expensed parts.

Software Install/Update/Removal (4)

Scope of Work: Installation, updating, or removal of up to four software applications. Verification of software functionality post-installation or removal.

System Restore

Scope of Work: Restoration of PC to an earlier version of Windows. Verification of system functionality post-restoration.

Virus/Malware Removal

Scope of Work: Identification and removal of malicious or bothersome programs. Preservation of the operating system and essential files.

Electrical help

Light Fixture Swap

Scope of Work: Assessment of existing light fixture. Replacement with a new light fixture. Testing for proper function and safety.

Furniture Assembly

Furniture Assembly Hourly Service Rate

Scope of Work: Evaluation of furniture assembly instructions. Assembly of small furniture units. Verification of stability and function.

Furniture Fixes

Furniture Repair Hourly Service Rate

Scope of Work: Evaluation of furniture damage. Repair procedures, including re-gluing, re-staining, or re-upholstering as needed.

Plumbing help

Bidet Accessory Install/Removal

Scope of Work: Assessment of toilet compatibility. Installation or removal of bidet attachment. Testing for proper function and no leaks.

Fixture Swap (Cosmetic)

Scope of Work: Identification of existing fixtures. Replacement with new sink faucet, shower head, or other cosmetic fixtures. Testing for proper function and no leaks.

Leak Under Sink Repair

Scope of Work: Identification of the source of the leak. Repair of minor leaks under the sink. Verification of successful repair with no further leaks.

Unclog Sink/Shower/Toilet (Minor)

Scope of Work: Assessment of the clog severity. Removal of non-major clogs. Verification of proper drainage post-service.
